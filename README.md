# Adidas-Sales-Analysis (Interactive Dashboard with Power BI)
## Project Objective:
Analyzing Adidas sales performance across the United States in 2020 & 2021.

## Dataset used
- <a href= "https://github.com/gulmirakalbayevna/Data-Analysis-Dashboard/blob/main/Adidas%20US%20Sales%20Datasets.xlsx">Dataset</a>

## Dashboard Overview
- KPI: Total Sales, Operating Profit, Units Sold, Price per Unit, and Operating Margin
- Sales Over Time: Monthly trend of Total Sales by Invoice Date
- Sales by State: Geographic distribution of sales across US states
- Sales by Region: Revenue breakdown by region
- Sales by Product: Total sales ranked by product category
- Sales by Retailer: Total sales ranked by retailer
- Slicers: Interactive filters for Region and Invoice Date

## Data Source
The report uses a dataset named Data Sales Adidas containing the following fields:

- Invoice Date — Date of sale
- State — US state of the transaction
- Region — Sales region
- Retailer — Retailer name
- Product — Product category
- Total Sales — Revenue generated
- Operating Profit — Profit after operating costs
- Operating Margin — Profitability ratio
- Units Sold — Number of units sold
- Price per Unit — Average selling price
